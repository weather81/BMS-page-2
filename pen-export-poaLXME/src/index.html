In order to protect and maintain voltage and current of the battery with in safe limit Battery Management System (BMS) should be used. BMS provides thermal management to the battery, safeguarding it against over and under temperature and also during short circuit conditions. The battery pack is designed with series connected cells produce 12V. The charging and releasing levels of the battery pack is indicated by interfacing the MSP430 microcontroller . The SOC of the battery is a vital information to the EV user. It can be displayed as the percentage of full charge capacity that is still available from the battery or be used to estimate the vehicle range based on additional information from previous driving cycles. However, it can not be measured directly from the battery and have a strong dependence with the temperature and the operating conditions
